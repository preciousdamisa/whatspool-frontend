Please read the information below:

To facilicate easy and quick funding of user accounts. WhatsPool makes use of an online payment gateway (Paystack). Paystack is a trusted online payment service provider.

Follow these steps to fund your WhatsPool account:

Step 1

Enter the amount you wish to fund your account with and click on the Proceed button above.

Step 2

After clicking on the button, you get a popup, where you're to confirm the transaction. Simply click on "Continue".

Step 3

Next, Paystack would show a popup, where you're to choose a payment method (Card or Transfer).

If you choose the Transfer method, ensure that you do not close your browser or the page until you're done with the transfer. After making the transfer to the account number specified, return to the page and click on the "I've sent the money" button.

Paystack would confirm your transfer and show a success page if it was successful.

Important! Whichever payment method (Card or Transfer) you use, ensure you don't close your browser or the page after seeing the Paystack success page. After the Paystack success page, you should also see the WhatsPool success page shown below, on which you can click on the Okay button to return back to the WhatsPool website.

If any of these steps is unclear or if you encounter any issue while attempting to fund your account, please send a WhatsApp message to {{supportLineOne}} or send an email to whatspoolgame@gmail.com
