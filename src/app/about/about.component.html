What is WhatsPool?

WhatsPool is an online platform that allows people answer questions and win prizes for answering correctly and quickly. It holds Monday to Saturday. From 9:00 P.M - 9:10 P.M.

Ten (10) questions for 10 minutes, with one hundred (100) persons having the chance to emerge as winners.

WhatsPool is all about FASTEST FINGERS, its all about SPEED. The questions are simple, general and intuitive, so everyone has an equal chance of winning. There are usually one hundred (100) winners from each competition. You qualify to be a winner if you're among the one hundred (100) persons to answer all the questions correctly (i.e scoring 10).

How do I participate?

To participate, follow these steps:

Ensure you have up to #100 in your main balance or as referral bonus, it is the required WhatsPool participation fee. You can find out your balance in the Account page

Step 1

On the Home page, or in the navigation menu, click on "Compete".

Step 2

Next, a page would be shown you to check your registration status and you would be taken to the appropriate page, based on the result. While registering, ensure you verify your personal information. Ensure it is current and correct, especially your phone number & E-Mail, as they are what would be used in contacting you, should you emerge a winner.

Step 3

After confirming your info. Click on the "Register" button, and you would be registered as a competitor for the next WhatsPool.

Step 4

On the date for WhatsPool, ensure you're logged in. Once it's time (9:00 PM), in the Home page or in the navigation menu, click on "Compete" and you would directed to the page where you can answer the questions and stand a chance of being a winner.

Should you emerge a winner, your name would be shown in the winners table in the Winners page. Which you can check immediately after the game (9:10 P.M). Yes! that's how fast the system determines the winners, WhatsPool is transparent in its selection of winners. So, you have no reason to ever doubt its integrity. You can also check your wins in the Account page, in the "Your Wins" section.

You would receive an E-Mail, from the WhatsPool team, with steps on how to claim your prize. Your prize money is sent to any Nigerian bank acount of your choice.

Recommendations

  • We recommend that you have a strong and stable internet connection, when participating in WhatsPool, since your chance of being a winner, greatly hinges on how fast, you're able to fetch and answer the questions.
  • On login, each user has a one (1) hour login session, before they're logged out automatically by the system. This is a security measure, to prevent an unauthorized user from accessing your account while you're away from your device.

    So, in order to avoid being logged out while the game is on, ensure you log in few minutes to the game time (e.g 8:50 P.M). This would give you enough time to participate in the game, without being automatically logged out by the system after one (1) hour.

    The above stated scenario is different from the scenario where the game time is up, while a participant hasn't finished. Once the game time (10 minutes) is up, a participant would be unable to provide further answers and the game ends automatically.
