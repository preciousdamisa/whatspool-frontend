Step 1

Go to the Account page and get your referral link by clicking on the "Copy Referral Link" button.

Step 2

Share the link along with an explanatory message. You can do this via the following ways:

  • Share your referral link on your social networks. E.g WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.
  • Send an SMS to your contacts with your referral link.
  • Send an email to your contacts with your referral link.

With that done, your friend clicks on your referral link, then signs up. The system links them to you as the referrer.

You get a #25 bonus for each referree. This money would accumulate and could be used in participating in WhatsPool.
